Courses

Competition

We participated in many tournaments, giving our students the opportunity to compete, teach and learn from their peers at the level of the country and the world. Competition is a great way for motivation and fast learning. Competitions build important interpersonal skills that prepare students for the workplace later in life. It also helps them succeed socially and academically in school.
